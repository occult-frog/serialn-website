add-recipe

Walks you through a proceedure to add a recipe to your collection. Requires a recipe name. Don't pass the recipe name as [RECIPENAME].csv. Pass it without the extension. When entering the recipe amount, enter it as 500 mL or 500mL. Entering only a number assigns no unit to that ingredient and leaving it blank assigns neither an amount nor a unit.

SYNTAX

add-recipe [RECIPENAME]

EXAMPLES

Creates a new recipe named recipe1

add-recipe recipe1
