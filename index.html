serialn
recipes in your terminal
serialn brings your recipes into the terminal. Add, scale, and search through your recipes all via the command line.

Features

Local Storage


Store recipes locally in the form of csv files.

Search


Search recipes by name and tags.

Scale


Scale any recipe by any number of times and get correct measurements automatically

Tags


Organize recipes by cuisine, diet, season, or any tag you want.

Commands

list-recipes


list-recipes


List all recipes from your collection in a neat and ordered manner.

view-recipe


view-recipe [RECIPENAME] [OPTIONS]


Display the full recipe scaled by 1 or more.

refresh-recipes


refresh-recipes


Sync and keep track of your recipe collection via a recipe index.

add-recipe


add-recipe [RECIPENAME]


Add a new recipe to your collection interactively.

remove-recipe


remove-recipe [RECIPENAME]


Remove a recipe from your collection permanently.

search-tags


search-tags [TAGS]


Search recipes across your collection by their tags.

add-tags


add-tags [OPTIONS] [RECIPENAME] [TAGS]


Add tags to your recipes to organize your collection.

edit-recipe


edit-recipe [RECIPENAME] [OPTIONS]


Edit parts of your recipes like ingredients, steps, and extra notes.
